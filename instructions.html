Instructions

Enjoy playing through the story by clicking one of the buttons from each page.

Pictures can trigger sound effects. Hover your mouse over the photo to activate a sound effect. not all images have an associated sound affect.

TTS has been implemented into this web site. Simply click on the text below the game page image regarding the plot. Now listen to your device read out the plot.

TTS and image audio effects can be disabled by clicking on the corresponding icon in the toolbar when playing the game.
